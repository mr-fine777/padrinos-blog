AdPass

Download AdPass, a completely free browser extension for both Firefox and Chrome, awaiting publishing from Chrome/Firefox for their webstore, you can download it here for free. AdPass uses a script inside of YouTube that handles any form of video speed, for example the 2x speed, and amplifies it to 16x speed, however only for the ads. I use this everyday, and decided to send it out for other people to use for free. Once they are accepted onto their respective stores I'll update the download buttons. It does come with an ad replacement script, simply to make some revenue. It will not effect youtube.com. If there's a website that has errors on it with the extension, please let me know at conway.bat@gmail.com

To install AdPass for Chrome, simply download the zip and unzip it in an isolated folder, then go to "chrome://extensions/" and turn on "Developer mode", then click "Load unpacked" and select the folder you downloaded AdPass to.
